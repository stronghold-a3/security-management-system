# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: June 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Stronghold A3 Security Agency ("we", "us", "our") operates the Security Management System (the "Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
- Name and contact details
- Login credentials
- Security clearance information
- Employment history

### 2.2 Usage Information
- IP addresses
- Browser type and version
- Pages visited and time spent
- Search queries
- Device information

### 2.3 Security Information
- Authentication logs
- Access logs
- Audit trail entries
- Security incident reports

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the Platform
- Monitor and improve security
- Comply with legal obligations
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Support customer service
- Generate compliance reports

## 4. Data Retention

- **Operational Data**: 3 years
- **Audit Logs**: 7 years (DOLE DO-174 requirement)
- **Backup Data**: 1 year
- **Session Data**: 30 days after inactivity

## 5. Data Security

We implement:
- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Role-based access control
- Regular security audits
- Automated threat detection
- Incident response procedures

## 6. Third-Party Sharing

We do NOT share personal data except:
- With authorized security personnel
- To comply with legal requirements
- To law enforcement when required by court order
- To service providers who are contractually bound

## 7. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
- Export your data
- Restrict processing
- Lodge a complaint

**To exercise these rights, email: privacy@strongholda3.com**

## 8. Data Processing Agreement

For enterprise clients, a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) is available. Contact legal@strongholda3.com.

## 9. International Transfers

Data is processed in the Philippines and may be transferred to authorized third parties in compliance with applicable laws.

We may update this policy periodically. Changes are effective when posted. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
